The article argues that finance teams typically inherit their payout infrastructure rather than deliberately choosing it. The real strategic distinction lies not in SWIFT versus local systems but in whether the settlement model, Real-Time Gross Settlement versus Deferred Net Settlement, aligns with each payment's actual needs.
