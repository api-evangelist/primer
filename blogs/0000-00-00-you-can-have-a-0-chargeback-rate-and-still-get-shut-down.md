Chargebacks cost merchants more than the dispute fee alone, including lost revenue, operational overhead, and the risk of losing the ability to process payments. This Payments Unfiltered interview with Qi Cao, CEO of Chargeblast, covers chargeback management, Visa's Acquiring Monitoring Program (VAMP), and why merchants should focus on business fundamentals rather than chasing a zero chargeback rate.
