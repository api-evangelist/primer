Primer introduces a finance suite of three tools, noting that 71% of finance teams spend over a quarter of their time on manual reconciliation workflows. Reconciliation automates matching settlement data from multiple PSPs into a unified ledger; Costs Overview gives real-time breakdowns of processor and interchange fees across 100+ data points; and Global Accounts lets teams control multi-currency conversions rather than leaving them to processors.
