The article examines why foreign exchange leakage persists for global businesses despite finance teams understanding FX risk. It argues the core issue is not poor rates but unnecessary conversions happening inside payment infrastructure before finance can intervene, and that controlling when and where currency conversion occurs is essential to natural hedging strategies.
