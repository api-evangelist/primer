This Payments Unfiltered episode features James Fry from Worldpay/Global Payments, who has spent over 10 years managing payments infrastructure at global scale. It discusses the hidden complexity behind a payment service provider, including the hundreds of annual card network changes, why no single PSP can serve all merchant needs, and the importance of flexibility in payments infrastructure.
