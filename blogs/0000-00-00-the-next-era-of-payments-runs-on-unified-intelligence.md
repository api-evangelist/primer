Primer announces a $100 million Series C funding round, framed around how unified payment infrastructure enables AI to function effectively by providing complete transaction data rather than fragmented information across multiple systems. CEO and co-founder Gabriel Le Roux argues this lets payments and finance teams move faster, catch problems earlier, and make smarter real-time decisions.
